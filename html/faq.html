Frequently Asked Questions

  • Question:
    What does ichor mean, and why did you choose this name?

  • Answer:
    In Greek it means “The fluid that flows like blood in the veins of the Gods”. Which means that its the blood of someone who lives forever, and we chose this name because your blood will be eternal when donating.
  • Question:
    Can I personally reach out to people on the app for blood?

  • Answer:
    To make sure that the bloodgiving prosess is safe, only the health care personell can reach out. This is a security messurment so that the app and the blood wont be misused.
  • Question:
    Can everyone be blood doner?

  • Answer:
    Everyone can apply to be donor, but u have to get through a health check to make sure that your blood is healthy enough to give.
  • Question:
    Do you have to pay?

  • Answer:
    Some healthposts may charge you a small amount of money for the health check, but there are also free options
  • Question:
    Where will this app work?

  • Answer:
    Our purpose is to make this available for the world, because it is not just an issue in some countries but worldwide. With the right connections with healtposts in the different countries, we may expand our app and our vision.
  • Question:
    Is there a way to apply if you do not have access to techonolgy?

  • Answer:
    Yes, you can visit our nearest health station, show your interest and become a doner. However, our purpose is to reach out to doners as fast as possible with the help of modern technology. That’s why the process might vary for people without access to technology.
